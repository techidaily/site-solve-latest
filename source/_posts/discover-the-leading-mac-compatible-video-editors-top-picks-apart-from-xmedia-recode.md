## #4\. Avidemux

Being an open source and free tool for video conversion and video editing, Avidemux can be served as another alternative to XMedia Recode on Mac. It supports many file types, including AVI, DVD compatible MPEG files, MP4 and ASF, using a variety of codecs. HEVC codec is also supported in Avidemux. 

Besides, Avidemux offers users numerous of basic edits, including cutting out a section out of a video file without re-encoding, cropping the frame size of the video, rotating in 90, 180 degree & flipping horizontally or vertically, and adding filters. It's best for the novice to make quick edits. Tasks can be automated using projects, job queue and powerful scripting.
