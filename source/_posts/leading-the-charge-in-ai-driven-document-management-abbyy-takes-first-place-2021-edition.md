### ABBYY named 2021 technology leader of Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)

The Quadrant Knowledge Solutions SPARK Matrix™ provides competitive analysis and ranking of the leading IDP vendors, including strategic information for users to evaluate different vendor capabilities, competitive differentiation, and market position.

In its 2021 SPARK Matrix, Quadrant Knowledge Solutions placed ABBYY in the leading position for IDP solutions, due to the strong ratings of its comprehensive technology for document processing and no-code IDP platform, ABBYY Vantage.

ABBYY’s out-of-the-box ML-based pre-trained document skills and pre-built connectors are developed by ABBYY and its partners and available in the ABBYY Marketplace. With its robust technology platform, comprehensive functional capabilities, and compelling product strategy and roadmap, ABBYY is well-positioned to expand its market share in the global intelligent document processing market.
